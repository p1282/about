거북이 1~100적기

1~100 숫자를 10개씩 10줄로 적는다.

입력

없음

출력

1~100 숫자

풀이


1~10 적기
import turtle

turtle.shape('turtle')
turtle.speed(1)
turtle.color('blue')

turtle.penup()

for i in range(1,11,1):
    turtle.write(i)
    turtle.dot()
    turtle.forward(30)

import turtle

turtle.shape('turtle')
turtle.speed(1)
turtle.color('blue')

turtle.penup()

for i in range(1,11,1):
    turtle.write(i)

turtle.goto(0,-30)

for i in range(11,21,1):
    turtle.write(i)
    turtle.dot()
    turtle.forward(30)

1~100 열줄에 적기
import turtle

turtle.shape('turtle')
turtle.speed(1)
turtle.color('blue')

turtle.penup()

y = 0
st =  1

for j in range(10):
    for i in range(st,st+10,1):
        turtle.write(i)
        turtle.dot()
        turtle.forward(30)

    y = y - 30
    turtle.goto(0,y)
    st = st + 10
